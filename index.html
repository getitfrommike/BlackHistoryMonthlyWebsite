About Black History MonthLY

Black History Monthly is more than a website—it's a FOUNDATION to preserve, explore, promote and amplify Black narratives of America from ancient empires to modern innovations.

We uncover the truth that has been hidden for over 500 years and show facts and evidence to set the record of the ancient Black history of America correct.

Spotlight legends, and create the new world through the greatness of Black men and women. Challenge the timeline we're taught. From the Americas and beyond, this is Black history—unfiltered and alive.
