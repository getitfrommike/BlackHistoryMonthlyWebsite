FAQ

What is Black History MonthLY?
Black History MonthLY is an ongoing archive and learning hub celebrating Black memory, enterprise, and community uplift — every month of the year.
Are you a 508(c)(1)(A) organization?
Yes. We operate as a 508(c)(1)(A) faith-and-education ministry. Donations support teaching, research, and community programs.
How can I contribute or get involved?
You can volunteer, submit research or figures, partner with a program, or donate. Visit the Contact page.
Can I suggest a figure for the archive?
Absolutely — send sources and a short write-up via the contact form. We prioritize verifiable, primary-source-based entries.
Can I use your materials in my class or church?
Most content is available for non-commercial educational use with attribution. For anything else, please reach out on the contact page.
